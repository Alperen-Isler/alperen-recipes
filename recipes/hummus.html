The perfect Hummus

Description

Self made Hummus that isn't just easy to make but also tastes really good and is really creamy. You could add any toppings you like. For example Tuna, ground beef, only olive oil or spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas (220g)
  • 3 tablespoons of Tahini
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 small cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon of Cumin powder
  • a few cubes of ice
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Drain the chickpeas and add them into a mixer.
  2. Add all the other ingredients into the mixer and let it mix until smooth and creamy. If it's not creamy enough for your liking you can add more ice cubes.
  3. Plate the Hummus and add toppings of your own liking.

Enjoy!
